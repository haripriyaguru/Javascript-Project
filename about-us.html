Our Story

Handcrafted with passion, designed with purpose

The Artisan Journey

Artisan was founded in 2015 by jewelry designer Emma Chen with a simple mission: to create beautiful, handcrafted jewelry that celebrates individuality and craftsmanship.

What began as a small studio in Emma's apartment has grown into a thriving business with a team of skilled artisans, but our core values remain the same. Each piece is still handcrafted with meticulous attention to detail, using traditional techniques alongside modern design sensibilities.

We believe that jewelry is more than just an accessory—it's a form of self-expression, a keepsake of special moments, and a connection to the artisan who created it. That's why we pour our hearts into every piece we make.

"I started Artisan because I wanted to create jewelry with soul—pieces that tell a story and connect people to the craft behind them."

GURUNATHAN

Founder & Creative Director

Our Values

Craftsmanship

We honor traditional jewelry-making techniques while embracing innovation. Each piece is handcrafted with precision and care by our skilled artisans.

Sustainability

We're committed to ethical sourcing and sustainable practices. From recycled metals to responsible gemstone sourcing, we strive to minimize our environmental impact.

Inclusivity

We create jewelry for everyone. Our designs celebrate diversity and individual expression, with pieces that can be treasured by people of all backgrounds.

Community

We believe in giving back. A portion of our proceeds supports artisan communities and jewelry education programs around the world.

Our Process

Each Artisan piece goes through a meticulous creation process, from initial concept to final polish.

1

Design

Our designers sketch concepts inspired by nature, architecture, and cultural motifs, refining each design until it achieves the perfect balance of beauty and wearability.

2

Material Selection

We carefully source ethically mined gemstones and recycled precious metals, selecting each material for its quality, sustainability, and beauty.

3

Crafting

Our skilled artisans use traditional techniques like lost-wax casting, filigree, and hand-forging to transform raw materials into intricate jewelry pieces.

4

Finishing

Each piece is meticulously polished, set, and inspected to ensure it meets our high standards of quality and craftsmanship before it reaches you.

Our Studio

Located in the heart of New York's jewelry district, our studio is where creativity and craftsmanship come together. It's a space filled with the sounds of hammering, filing, and polishing—the soundtrack of jewelry being made by hand.

We've designed our studio to honor traditional craftsmanship while embracing modern tools and techniques. Each workstation is equipped with both centuries-old tools and cutting-edge technology, allowing our artisans to create pieces that blend the best of both worlds.

While we've grown over the years, we've maintained the intimate, collaborative atmosphere of our original workshop. Our artisans work closely together, sharing knowledge and inspiring each other to push the boundaries of their craft.
