Our Commitment to Sustainability

Creating beautiful jewelry with respect for people and planet

Our Approach

At Artisan, sustainability isn't just a buzzword—it's woven into every aspect of our business. We believe that beautiful jewelry shouldn't come at the expense of our planet or the people who help create it.

Our commitment to sustainability is an ongoing journey. We're constantly evaluating our practices, seeking out more responsible materials, and finding ways to reduce our environmental footprint while maximizing our positive social impact.

We believe in transparency and accountability. That's why we're sharing our sustainability initiatives with you—and why we welcome your feedback as we continue to improve.

Responsible Materials

Recycled Metals

We use 100% recycled gold and silver in our jewelry, reducing the need for environmentally damaging mining practices while maintaining the highest quality standards.

Our recycled metals come from post-consumer sources like old jewelry and industrial scrap, which are refined back to their pure form.

Ethical Gemstones

We carefully source our gemstones from suppliers who adhere to responsible mining practices and fair labor standards.

For colored gemstones, we prioritize stones that are traceable to their source and mined with minimal environmental impact. When possible, we use lab-grown alternatives that offer the same beauty without the mining footprint.

Sustainable Packaging

Our packaging is made from FSC-certified recycled paper and cardboard, printed with soy-based inks, and designed to be either recyclable or biodegradable.

We've eliminated plastic from our packaging and shipping materials, using innovative alternatives like compostable mailers and plant-based cushioning.

Certifications

We're proud to be certified by the Responsible Jewellery Council (RJC), which holds us to rigorous standards for responsible business practices.

Our diamonds are certified by the Kimberley Process, ensuring they are conflict-free, and we're working toward full traceability for all our materials.

Sustainable Production

Our commitment to sustainability extends to how we make our jewelry:

  • Energy Efficiency: Our studio is powered by 100% renewable energy, and we've invested in energy-efficient equipment and lighting.
  • Water Conservation: We use closed-loop water systems in our production processes to minimize water usage and prevent pollution.
  • Waste Reduction: We collect and recycle metal dust and scraps, ensuring nothing is wasted. Even our polishing compounds are environmentally friendly.
  • Chemical Management: We've eliminated toxic chemicals from our processes, using safer alternatives for cleaning and finishing our jewelry.
  • Handcrafted Approach: By prioritizing handcrafting over mass production, we reduce energy consumption and create pieces that are made to last.

Social Responsibility

Fair Labor Practices

We provide fair wages, safe working conditions, and professional development opportunities for all our artisans. We also ensure that our suppliers adhere to these same standards.

Our team members receive comprehensive benefits, including health insurance, paid time off, and parental leave. We believe that taking care of our people is essential to creating beautiful jewelry.

Artisan Training

We're committed to preserving traditional jewelry-making techniques by training the next generation of artisans. Our apprenticeship program provides hands-on education in metalsmithing and gemstone setting.

We also partner with local schools to offer workshops and internships, introducing young people to the craft of jewelry making.

Community Giving

We donate 5% of our profits to organizations that support environmental conservation and artisan communities around the world.

Our team members receive paid time off for volunteering, and we organize regular community service events to give back to our local community.

Diversity & Inclusion

We're committed to creating an inclusive workplace where diversity is celebrated. We actively work to ensure our team, marketing, and designs reflect and respect people of all backgrounds.

We also partner with organizations that promote diversity in the jewelry industry, providing mentorship and opportunities to underrepresented groups.

Transparency Report

We believe in being transparent about our sustainability journey—both our achievements and the challenges we face.

2022 Highlights

  • Achieved 100% recycled metals in all our jewelry
  • Reduced carbon emissions by 35% compared to 2020 baseline
  • Eliminated single-use plastics from our packaging and shipping
  • Launched our artisan apprenticeship program with 5 participants
  • Donated $50,000 to environmental and social causes

Challenges & Next Steps

While we made significant progress in 2022, we still face challenges in:

  • Tracing the origin of all colored gemstones
  • Reducing the environmental impact of shipping
  • Scaling our sustainable practices as we grow

In 2023, we're focusing on these areas while continuing to build on our achievements.
